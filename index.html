Our Story

Crafted with Passion

Prestige Confectionery began with a simple dream: to create cakes that don't just taste divine, but tell a story. Founded by Osundeyi Priscillia, we've grown from a home kitchen to a beloved brand trusted by hundreds of families and businesses across Lagos.

What sets us apart? Every cake is handcrafted with premium ingredients, attention to detail, and a genuine love for celebrations. We don't just bake — we create centerpieces for your most precious moments.

From intimate birthday gatherings to grand weddings and corporate galas, Prestige Confectionery brings sweetness, elegance, and unforgettable flavor to every occasion.
